Introduction

Taiwan Company Blocker is a Chrome extension that helps you filter out companies or job listings you don't want to see on job platforms. Just set your own keywords to block specific content. It also supports wildcards and regular expressions, giving you flexible control over your rules. If you'd rather not configure everything yourself, you can subscribe to filter lists curated by others.

Tired of seeing notorious or irrelevant companies over and over again on job sites? Taiwan Company Blocker helps you get rid of that noise and makes your job search experience cleaner and more focused.

Support Platforms

  • TaiwanJobs (台灣就業通)

Features

Click to Block Companies or Jobs

Once the extension is enabled, a block button will appear in the top-right corner of job listings. Click it to add the job to your blocklist.

Manually Edit Blocking Rules

You can freely edit rules for company names and job titles to decide which companies or jobs to block.

You can use wildcards `*` to match zero or more characters, or use regular expressions (regex) to create more complex rules.

Support Multiple Blocking Methods

Subscribe to Rules

Subscribe to rule lists provided by others, which will automatically fetch updates from the URL daily.

You can also check out the company subscription list maintained by the extension author to quickly filter industries like gambling or outsourcing.

Quick Company Search Shortcut

Provides commonly used search keywords at the bottom of the blocking window. Click to quickly search on Google.
